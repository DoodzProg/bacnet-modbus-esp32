BMS Gateway v1.1.0

Industrial Modbus TCP to BACnet/IP Gateway

Installation Guide:

1. Connect your ESP32-S3 (8MB Flash minimum) to your PC via USB.
2. Click the Connect button below.
3. Select your USB serial port from the pop-up menu.
4. Click "Install BMS Gateway" and wait for the process to complete.

Supported browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge.
